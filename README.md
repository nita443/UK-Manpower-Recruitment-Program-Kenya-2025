## Project Overview
This project involves the design and execution of an international manpower recruitment program aimed at addressing critical labour shortages in the United Kingdom. The assignment focuses on sourcing, recruiting, and facilitating the relocation of skilled and semi-skilled talent from Kenya for a UK-based client.

The program is structured to deliver a compliant, job-ready workforce while ensuring ethical recruitment practices and candidate welfare throughout the recruitment and relocation lifecycle.

---

## Project Scope & Objectives
- Establish and manage an end-to-end recruitment pipeline for UK labour needs  
- Ensure full compliance with:
  - UK immigration laws
  - UK labour regulations
  - Kenyan labour and ethical recruitment standards  
- Facilitate smooth relocation and transition for successful candidates  
- Strengthen employer branding for the client in the Kenyan labour market  

---

## Stakeholder Engagement
The project required coordination across multiple stakeholders, including:

- UK-based HR and operations teams  
- Legal and immigration consultants in Kenya and the UK  
- Recruited candidates throughout the sourcing, selection, and relocation stages  

Continuous communication was maintained to provide progress updates, manage expectations, and address operational or regulatory challenges.

---

## Key Activities & Responsibilities

### Candidate Sourcing & Screening
- Developed targeted sourcing strategies, including local partnerships and digital recruitment campaigns  
- Conducted pre-screening to ensure suitability and readiness  

### Interview & Selection Coordination
- Managed interview scheduling and logistics  
- Ensured fair, transparent, and structured selection processes  
- Coordinated feedback loops between candidates and client stakeholders  

### Documentation & Compliance
- Oversaw collection, verification, and submission of:
  - Employment documentation
  - Visa and immigration documents  
- Ensured adherence to Kenyan and UK labour and immigration laws  

### Relocation Logistics
- Coordinated pre-departure briefings  
- Supported travel arrangements and initial accommodation  
- Provided guidance on settlement and transition requirements  

### Candidate Support
- Maintained continuous candidate engagement  
- Provided clear communication on recruitment stages, timelines, and relocation processes  

---

## Challenges & Risk Mitigation

### Visa Processing Delays
**Challenge:** Delays and changes in UK immigration policies  
**Mitigation:**  
- Maintained close collaboration with immigration consultants  
- Kept up-to-date with policy changes  
- Managed candidate expectations through transparent communication  

### Candidate Readiness & Integration
**Challenge:** Cultural adaptation and workplace readiness post-arrival  
**Mitigation:**  
- Implemented pre-departure orientation sessions  
- Covered cultural norms, workplace expectations, and settling-in guidance

## Metrics & Outcomes
- Successful placements: Ongoing program metric  
- Time-to-fill: Reduced compared to industry averages  
- Visa approval rate: High approval success rate achieved  
- Candidate satisfaction: Strong satisfaction levels reported post-relocation  
- Compliance: Full compliance achieved in internal and external audits
